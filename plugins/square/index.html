Make the low end mono-proof.

Square
WARREN LABS  ·  MONO-COMPATIBILITY + PHASE METERING

Sums the bass to mono below a crossover, auditions the result, and shows you the stereo field — goniometer and correlation — so the low end holds up everywhere it's played.

  • Bass-mono below an adjustable crossover
  • Goniometer + correlation meter
  • Solo-low + full mono fold
  • VST3 / AU / Standalone
Installing the beta

This beta isn’t notarized by Apple yet, so macOS shows a one-time “unidentified developer” warning the first time you open the installer. It’s safe to allow — here’s how:

  • macOS 15 (Sequoia) and later — double-click the downloaded .pkg. When macOS blocks it, open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway.
  • macOS 14 and earlier — right-click the .pkg → Open → Open.

Installs to /Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/Square.component (AU), …/VST3/Square.vst3 (VST3), and /Applications/Square.app (Standalone). Relaunch your DAW afterward so it rescans. A notarized build with no warning ships once the Warren Labs developer account is approved.
